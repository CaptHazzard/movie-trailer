Back to The Future

Marty McFly, a high school student in Hill Valley, takes an unexpected trip back in time with a machine built from a modified DeLorean by his friend Doc Brown. After being transported back to 1955, Marty must find a way to repair the time machine and return to his time. But after endangering his own future on the way to find the Doc of the past, he must also fix his interference and ensure his own existence. After making sure his parents once again meet each other and fall in love, Doc Brown harnesses a lightning strike predicted by a library flyer from 1985 and sends Marty and the repaired time machine back to 1985.
